INCIDENT DATE: 2020-11-27 13:55
INCIDENT LOCATION: 1100 block of W 30th St, Los Angeles, CA 90007
INCIDENT OFF CAMPUS: True
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: The victim was selling a laptop computer online and agreed to meet the suspect to complete the transaction. The suspect was seated in a vehicle. During the transaction, she began to drive off with the victim’s computer. The victim held onto the computer and was dragged for approximately 20 feet. The suspect drove away with the victim’s property eastbound on 30th Street, then south on McClintock Avenue.
SUSPECT DESCRIPTION: Black female, approximately 20-25 years old, 5 feet 3 inches to 5 feet 6 inches and weighing 250 pounds. She had braided hair with blonde highlights. She was wearing an orange and gray tie-dye shirt.
SUSPECT VEHICLE: Newer red four-door Nissan or Toyota sedan.
