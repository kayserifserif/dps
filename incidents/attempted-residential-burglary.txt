Attempted Residential Burglary
2020-06-05T19:45:02-07:00
https://dps.usc.edu/attempted-residential-burglary/

ALERT TW#: 2020-011
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: The male suspect attempted to break into the victim’s (former USC student) residence through a back door but was interrupted by the victim. The suspect then jumped over the backyard fence and fled on a bicycle.
DATE & TIME OF OCCURRENCE: June 5, 2020 at 3:50 p.m.
LOCATION: 2600 Block of South Orchard Avenue (off-campus)
SUSPECT DESCRIPTION: The suspect is a black male who wore a black long-sleeved shirt, gray pants and a green backpack.
SUSPECT’S BICYCLE: The suspect rode a black “fixie” bicycle with a black front rim and a chrome rear rim.
