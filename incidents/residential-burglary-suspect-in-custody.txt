SUMMARY: Residential Burglary (Suspect in Custody)
POST DATE: 2020-08-10T14:59:27-07:00
URL: https://dps.usc.edu/residential-burglary-suspect-in-custody/
IRN #: 2020-06

INCIDENT DATE: 2020-07-30 03:30
INCIDENT LOCATION: S Figueroa St & W 23rd St, Los Angeles, CA 90007
INCIDENT OFF CAMPUS: True
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: On July 30, 2020 at 3:30 a.m., the suspect entered the residence through an unlocked front door and removed a skateboard, gym bag and other property while the victims were sleeping. A few days later, after realizing they had been burglarized, the victims reviewed their security camera footage and observed the suspect entering and removing property.

ARREST DATE: 2020-08-07 15:00
DISPOSITION: On August 7, 2020, while on patrol, a DPS officer detained a person riding a bicycle who fit the description of the suspect identified in this burglary. Upon completing their investigation, the suspect was arrested and taken into custody.
