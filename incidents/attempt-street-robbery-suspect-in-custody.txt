SUMMARY: Attempt Street Robbery (Suspect in Custody)
POST DATE: 2020-08-20T12:57:48-07:00
URL: https://dps.usc.edu/attempt-street-robbery-suspect-in-custody/
IRN #: 2020-07

INCIDENT DATE: 2020-08-19 17:40
INCIDENT LOCATION: S Figueroa St & W 28th St, Los Angeles, CA 90007
INCIDENT OFF CAMPUS: True
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: On August 19, 2020 at 5:40 p.m., the suspect approached the victim on foot, demanded the victim’s money and property while simulating a weapon. The suspect fled without the victim’s property.

ARREST DATE: 2020-08-19 17:42
DISPOSITION: On August 19, 2020, at 5:42 p.m., while on patrol, DPS and LAPD officers, observed the suspect running south on Figueroa Street, detained him. Upon completing their investigation, the suspect was positively identified by the victim and he was arrested and taken into custody.
