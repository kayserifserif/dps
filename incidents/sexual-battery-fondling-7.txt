FIRST INCIDENT: Reporting party, a USC student, reported that as she was leaving campus from the McCarthy Way entrance after her class ended at Fertitta Hall, she crossed eastbound across Figueroa Street. When she reached the driveway leading to the Figueroa parking structure near Rosso Oro’s Pizza, she heard a bike coming from behind her. Reporting party turned around and then felt a hard slap to her buttocks. Reporting party said the suspect rode away southbound on the east sidewalk and out of sight.

LOCATION: USC Hotel, 3540 Figueroa St. (on campus)
SUSPECT DESCRIPTION: Male, Hispanic, age 20, long black shoulder length hair, wearing a gray hoodie and a backpack.
SUSPECT BIKE DESCRIPTION: Dark colored bicycle
