ALERT CA#: 2020-014
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: The victim, not affiliated with USC, stated that he was standing at the bus stop at the intersection of Adams Avenue and Magnolia Street when he was approached by two suspects on foot. One suspect displayed a handgun and demanded the victim’s property in Spanish. After taking the victim’s money, the suspects ran away westbound on Adams Avenue.
DATE AND TIME OF OCCURRENCE: June 26, 2020 at approximately 6 a.m.
LOCATION: Bus stop at the southwest corner of Adams Avenue and Magnolia Street (off-campus)
DESCRIPTION OF SUSPECTS: First Suspect was described as a Hispanic male, 5’10” to 6’00” tall, 20-22-years-old and wearing an orange hoodie. Second Suspect was described as a Hispanic male, 5’10” to 6’00” tall, 20-22-years-old and wearing a black hoodie with white stripes down the sleeves.
SUSPECT VEHICLE: No vehicle was seen.
