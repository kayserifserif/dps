Residential Burglary (Update)
2020-07-20T18:38:31-07:00
https://dps.usc.edu/residential-burglary-2/

ALERT TW#: 2020-015
DESCRIPTION OF INCIDENT: The victim was inside her apartment when she observed the suspect coming out of the bedroom closet, armed with a pocket-knife. The suspect fled with the victim’s cell phone. DPS, along with LAPD officers, cordoned off and conducted a search of the entire building. The suspect was not found.
DATE & TIME OF OCCURRENCE: July 20, 2020, 4:03 p.m.
LOCATION: University Gateway Apartments, 3335 S. Figueroa St. (off-campus)
SUSPECT DESCRIPTION: Male, Black, approximately 20-25 years, blue and gray wind breaker, dark pants. Victim later reported that the suspect had a thin, faded tattoo across his forehead, another tattoo under both his right and left eye, and possibly wearing a Los Angeles Trade Technical College (LATCC) nametag with the name “Ramon”.
SUSPECT VEHICLE: No vehicle was seen.
