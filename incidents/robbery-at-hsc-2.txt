2018-06-07T21:36:46-07:00

ALERT #: TW2018-010
REPORTED OFFENSE: Robbery
DATE & TIME OF OCCURRENCE: June 6, 2018 at 11:43 p.m.
LOCATION: 1500 Block of Soto Street, between Norfolk Street and Alcazar Street, L.A. (adjacent to Health Sciences Campus)
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: Victim, not affiliated with the university, was standing at a taco truck attempting to purchase food when approached by the suspect. The suspect displayed a handgun and demanded the victim’s property. The victim gave the suspect money and the suspect fled on foot with the victim’s property. The victim was not injured.
SUSPECT DESCRIPTION: Suspect 1: Male, Hispanic, approximately 28 to 30 years old, wearing grey tank top and light colored shorts.
SUSPECT VEHICLE: None seen.
