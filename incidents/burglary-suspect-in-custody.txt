Incident: Burglary-Suspect in Custody
Date/Time: April 2, 2019, 5:28 AM
Location: Gateway Apartments, 3335 South Figueroa Street (off campus)
Description of incident: On April 3, 2019, a student was contacted by an unidentified male via social media who offered to return her stolen wallet for a fee. The student had not reported the wallet theft to DPS. The student agreed and during the return of her property, the student secretly photographed the unidentified male. The student then contacted DPS.
