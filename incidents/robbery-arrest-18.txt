INCIDENT DATE: 2018-03-25 03:32
INCIDENT LOCATION: Adams Blvd from Flower St, Los Angeles, CA 90007
INCIDENT OFF CAMPUS: True
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: On Sunday morning, March 25, 2018, at 3:32 AM, a female student was walking eastbound on Adams Blvd from Flower St. (toward The Lorenzo Apartments) when she was pushed to the ground and her purse forcibly taken from her. The suspect then fled westbound toward Figueroa and out of view.

ARREST DATE: 2018-03-26 14:30
ARREST LOCATION: S Figueroa St & W 30th St, Los Angeles, CA 90007
ARREST OFF CAMPUS: True
DISPOSITION: At approximately 2:30 PM on Monday, March 26, 2018, an LAPD patrol officer, provided with a suspect description by DPS, stopped and detained a man who matched the description of the suspect in the robbery. The man was taken into custody and was later positively identified by the victim as the suspect. The suspect remains in the custody of the LAPD.
