Sexual Battery (Suspect in Custody)
2020-02-12T19:03:50-08:00
https://dps.usc.edu/sexual-battery-suspect-in-custody-3/

Date & Time of Original Incident: Dec. 10, 2019 at approximately 10:30 a.m.
Location: Jefferson Boulevard west of Hill Street (off-campus)
Description of Incident: The victim (staff member) reported that a male suspect approached her from behind and exposed himself while she walked on Jefferson Boulevard between Hill Street and Grand Avenue. The suspect also inappropriately touched the victim before running southbound on Broadway Street.
Incident Resolution: On Feb. 12, 2020, the Los Angeles Police Department notified DPS that the suspect had been arrested after committing a similar offense in downtown Los Angeles. The suspect remains in custody.
