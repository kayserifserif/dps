Robbery Update
2021-02-10T15:33:13-08:00
https://dps.usc.edu/robbery-update/

Incident description: A student reported that she and her boyfriend were beaten and robbed after stopping at a gas station.
Incident update: Detectives from the Los Angeles Police Department and USC’s Department of Public Safety determined that this robbery did not occur in the USC area. According to the victims, this incident occurred a significant distance away from USC near the intersection of Olympic Boulevard and Fairfax Avenue in the mid-Wilshire area of Los Angeles.
Time & date of incident: 1:30 a.m. on Feb. 8, 2021
Location: Off campus at Amin’s Oil station at 2603 S. Normandie Ave.
