Incident description: The victim was sitting in her apartment when she heard a loud noise. She was then confronted by the suspect who had forced open the front door. Location: Off-campus in the 1100 block of Exposition Boulevard.
Suspect description: Male, Hispanic, age 20-25, 5 feet 9 inches tall, slim build, wearing a dark blue hooded sweatshirt over a white shirt and dark jeans.
