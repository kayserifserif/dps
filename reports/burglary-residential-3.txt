INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: After reporting missing property to building security, a review of recorded camera images revealed an unknown suspect trying door handles on the student’s floor. SUSPECT DESCRIPTION: Male, possibly Hispanic, 5’5”, 250 lbs., 30-40 years, curly hair, wearing a dark long sleeve shirt, dark shorts, green tennis shoes, wrapped in light gray blanket, carrying a skateboard.
SUSPECT VEHICLE: No vehicle involvement observed.
