INCIDENT DATE: 2020-07-20 16:03
INCIDENT LOCATION: University Gateway Apartments, 3335 S Figueroa St, Los Angeles, CA 90007
INCIDENT OFF CAMPUS: True
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: The victim was inside her apartment when she observed the suspect coming out of the bedroom closet, armed with a pocket-knife. The suspect fled with the victim’s cell phone. DPS, along with LAPD officers, cordoned off and conducted a search of the entire building. The suspect was not found.
SUSPECT DESCRIPTION: Male, Black, approximately 20-25 years, blue and gray wind breaker, dark pants. Victim later reported that the suspect had a thin, faded tattoo across his forehead, another tattoo under both his right and left eye, and possibly wearing a Los Angeles Trade Technical College (LATCC) nametag with the name “Ramon”.
SUSPECT VEHICLE: No vehicle was seen.
