SUMMARY: Aggravated Assault-Suspect in Custody
POST DATE: 2021-02-20T10:16:56-08:00
URL: https://dps.usc.edu/aggravated-assault-suspect-in-custody/

INCIDENT DATE: 2021-02-18 18:00
INCIDENT LOCATION: 3001 Figueroa St, Los Angeles, CA 90007
INCIDENT OFF CAMPUS: True
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: As a student was walking by the location, a transient stabbed her in the leg in an unprovoked attack. The student was transported to a local hospital where she was treated and released and reported to be in good condition.

RESOLUTION: Officers from the Los Angeles Police Department (LAPD) were nearby and immediately took the suspect into custody. The suspect was arrested and transported to LAPD for booking on aggravated assault charges.
