INCIDENT DATE: 2018-04-16 20:30
INCIDENT LOCATION: West sidewalk of S Figueroa St south of W 32nd St, Los Angeles, CA 90007
INCIDENT OFF CAMPUS: True
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: On April 16, 2018, at 8:30pm, the victim (USC student) walked out of Blaze Pizza on Figueroa Street and was approached by the suspect who was on a bicycle. The suspect told the victim he had a gun and demanded money. The victim gave the suspect cash and the suspect rode away on the bicycle northbound on Figueroa Street.

DISPOSITION: DPS detectives, working with investigators from the LAPD, identified and arrested Charles Harvey, a 62 year old male transient, for robbery. Harvey is in the custody of the LAPD where he is being held for a violation of his parole and pending robbery charges.
