REPORTED OFFENSE: Assault with a Deadly Weapon
INCIDENT DATE: 2018-09-10 19:15
INCIDENT LOCATION: McClintock Ave north of Jefferson Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90007
INCIDENT OFF CAMPUS: False
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: In an apparent act of road rage, the suspect driver pointed a handgun from the window of her car at the driver of the other involved vehicle. The other involved vehicle was a rideshare car transporting a student passenger. No shots were fired and there were no injuries. The passenger in the suspect vehicle also exited the car and threw food at the rideshare driver. The suspect vehicle was last seen traveling westbound on Jefferson from McClintock.
SUSPECT DESCRIPTION: Driver: Female, Black, 35-40 years of age, wearing a burgundy colored top.
SUSPECT DESCRIPTION: Passenger: Female, Black, 35-40 years of age, approximately 6 feet tall, thin build, wearing reddish-purple colored clothes.
SUSPECT VEHICLE: Burgundy colored Kia with Car Pro white paper plates with blue writing.
