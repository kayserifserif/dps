INCIDENT DATE: 2020-12-13 23:30
INCIDENT LOCATION: 1100 block of Exposition Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90037
INCIDENT OFF CAMPUS: True
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: The victim was sitting in her apartment when she heard a loud noise. She was then confronted by the suspect who had forced open the front door. The suspect grabbed a laptop from the victim and smashed it on the floor before kicking a large hole in a bedroom door down the hall. The suspect then abruptly walked out of the apartment without taking anything. The victim was not injured.
SUSPECT DESCRIPTION: Male, Hispanic, age 20-25, 5 feet 9 inches tall, slim build, wearing a dark blue hooded sweatshirt over a white shirt and dark jeans.
SUSPECT VEHICLE: None seen
