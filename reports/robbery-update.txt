INCIDENT DATE: 2021-02-08 01:30
INCIDENT LOCATION: Amin’s Oil, 2603 S Normandie Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90007
INCIDENT OFF CAMPUS: True
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: A student reported that she and her boyfriend were beaten and robbed after stopping at a gas station.

UPDATE: Detectives from the Los Angeles Police Department and USC’s Department of Public Safety determined that this robbery did not occur in the USC area. According to the victims, this incident occurred a significant distance away from USC near the intersection of Olympic Boulevard and Fairfax Avenue in the mid-Wilshire area of Los Angeles.
