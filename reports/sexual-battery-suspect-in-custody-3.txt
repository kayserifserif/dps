INCIDENT DATE: 2019-12-10 10:30
INCIDENT LOCATION: W Jefferson Blvd west of S Hill St, Los Angeles, CA 90007
INCIDENT OFF CAMPUS: True
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: The victim (staff member) reported that a male suspect approached her from behind and exposed himself while she walked on Jefferson Boulevard between Hill Street and Grand Avenue. The suspect also inappropriately touched the victim before running southbound on Broadway Street.

RESOLUTION: On Feb. 12, 2020, the Los Angeles Police Department notified DPS that the suspect had been arrested after committing a similar offense in downtown Los Angeles. The suspect remains in custody.
