REPORTED OFFENSE: Robbery-Suspect in Custody
INCIDENT DATE: 2019-08-17 08:44
INCIDENT LOCATION: W 32nd St & Hoover Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90007
INCIDENT OFF CAMPUS: True
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: On the morning of August 17, 2019, a student walking north bound on University Ave, was approached by a male suspect riding a bicycle. The suspect blocked the student’s path, displayed a handgun and demanded the student’s cell phone. The student complied and the suspect fled west bound on 32nd Street.

DISPOSITION: The suspect was later apprehended by the LAPD Gang Unit during an investigation at the suspect’s residence. The student’s cell phone was recovered.
