SUMMARY: Robbery Suspect in Custody
POST DATE: 2021-03-15T12:59:47-07:00
URL: https://dps.usc.edu/robbery-suspect-in-custody-3/

INCIDENT DATE: 2021-03-14 20:00
INCIDENT LOCATION: 3335 S Figueroa St, Los Angeles, CA 90007
INCIDENT OFF CAMPUS: True
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: The suspect entered the CVS pharmacy, took a pair of jumper cables and tried to leave without paying. As the suspect was leaving the store, a store employee confronted her about taking the cables without paying for them. The suspect threatened the employee with a cane taken from a store display and left the store with the jumper cables.

RESOLUTION: Officers from the Department of Public Safety arrived at the location and took the suspect into custody. The suspect was arrested and transported to the Los Angeles Police Department for booking on robbery charges.
