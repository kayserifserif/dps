INCIDENT DATE: 2021-02-08 01:30
INCIDENT LOCATION: Amin’s Oil, 2603 Normandie Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90007
INCIDENT OFF CAMPUS: True
INCIDENT DESCRIPTION: The suspects approached the victims as they were pumping gas. The first suspect punched the male victim numerous times, causing him to fall to the ground. Several additional suspects approached the victim while he was on the ground, and battered him. The second victim, a USC student, was approached by a female suspect, who punched her in the face. The suspects took the first victim’s smartphone and jewelry, and got into several vehicles and drove away in unknown directions. The victims were not able to provide descriptions of the other suspects.
SUSPECT DESCRIPTION: Black male, approximately 20 to 30 years old, 5 feet, 10 inches tall, with a long ponytail. 
SUSPECT DESCRIPTION: Black female, approximately 25 to 30 years old.
SUSPECT VEHICLE: No vehicle descriptions were available.
